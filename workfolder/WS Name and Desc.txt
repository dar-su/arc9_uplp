< [ARC9] PolyArms Project >

A stylish, low-poly, fast-paced, heavily customizable, and most of all, [b]FUN[/b] weapon pack!
Made using custom assets and pure love, all you need is the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2910505837] ARC9 base! [/url]
[img]https://i.imgur.com/b8BXpUZ.png[/img]
[img]https://i.imgur.com/yktdeQK.png[/img]

[h2]Some notes:[/h2] [list]
[*] This addon has tons of weapons and attachments that are [b]MY personal tastes[/b]!
[*] All weapons include support for [b]Stickers[/b] and [b]Charms[/b] (unfortunately, no camos).
[*] Includes C_Arms from [b]UnfixedCrayfish Low Poly Arms Rig[/b] and [b]Hozq Rig V4[/b]. Use the [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2257795841] Enhanced PlayerModel Selector Fesiug's Edit [/url] or similar addon to utilize them. They also have customizable sleeves and gloves.
[*] [b][url=https://steamcommunity.com/id/shortnamesalex/myworkshopfiles/?appid=4000] Maps used in screenshots. [/url][/b] 
[*] Most of the addon's size are of the [i]models[/i].
[*] If your attachments disappear when you install them, you might have too many addons installed. Disable or unsubscribe from a few of them, then restart.
[*] If you are having issues in multiplayer, don't forget to disable physical bullets, and set the tickrate to 66 or above.
[*] If you dislike the suppressor overheat mechanic, then copy this into the console:
[b]arc9_modifiers “OverheatOverride\tfalse”;arc9_modifiers_invalidateall[/b]
[/list]
